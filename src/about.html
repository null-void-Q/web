Mission Statement

The mission of Hogwarts School for Palestinians is to provide a safe haven where everyone is valued and all staff, in partnership with parents and families, is committed to doing what it takes for students to achieve academic success, as guided by state standards. Students are empowered to meet future challenges and to develop social awareness, civic responsibility, and personal growth so they will be active and informed participants in the global community.

Typical day at school

A typical day at school starts at 8:00 AM for all grades and contains eight classes of 40 minutes each and two breaks of 20 minutes, one between the 3rd and 4th classes, and the other between the 6th and 7th classes.

Regular school's vacations

The regular school's vacations are Fridays and Sundays of each week.
